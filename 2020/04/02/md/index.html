💡晶体管放大电路的三种接法
发表于 2020-04-02| 更新于 2020-05-22
阅读量:

一、基本共集放大电路

静态分析

\(\left\{\begin{matrix}
V_{BB}=I_{BQ}R_{b}+U_{BEQ}+I_{EQ}R_{e}\\V_{CC}=U_{CEQ}+I_{EQ}R_{e}
\end{matrix}\right.\)

\(\therefore I_{BQ}=\frac {V_{BB}-U_{BEQ}} {R_{b}+(1+\beta)R_{e}},I_{EQ}=(1+\beta)I_{BQ},U_{CEQ}=V_{CC}-I_{EQ}R_{e}\)

动态分析

\(\dot{A_{u}}=\frac{\dot{U_{o}}}{\dot{U_{i}}}=\frac{\dot{I_{e}}R_{e}}{\dot{I_{b}}(R_{b}+r_{be})+\dot{I_{e}}R_{e}}=\frac{(1+\beta)R_{e}}{R_{b}+r_{be}+(1+\beta)R_{e}}\)

\(R_{i}=\frac {U_{i}}{I_{i}}=\frac{I_{b}[R_{b}+r_{be}+(1+\beta)R_{e}]}{I_{b}}=R_{b}+r_{be}+(1+\beta)R_{e}\)
带负载后\(R_{i}=R_{b}+r_{be}+(1+\beta)(R_{e}//R_{L})\)
\(R_{o}=\frac{U_{o}}{I_{o}}=\frac{U_{o}}{I_{R_{e}}+I_{e}}=\frac{U_{o}}{\frac{U_{o}}{R_e}+(1+\beta)\frac{U_{o}}{R_b+r_{be}}}=R_e//\frac{R_b+r_{be}}{1+\beta}\)
带负载后\(R_{o}=R_e//\frac{R_b+r_{be}+R_s}{1+\beta}\)

二、基本共基放大电路

静态分析

\(\left\{\begin{matrix}
U_{BEQ}+I_{EQ}Re=V_{BB}\\I_{CQ}R_c+U_{CEQ}-U_{BEQ}=V_{CC}
\end{matrix}\right.\)

\(\therefore I_{EQ}=\frac {V_{BB}-U_{BEQ}} {R_e},I_{BQ}=\frac{I_{EQ}}{1+\beta}\)

动态分析

\(\dot{A_u}=\frac{\dot{U_o}}{\dot{U_i}}=\frac{\dot{I_c}R_c}{\dot{I_e}R_e+\dot{I_b}r_{be}}=\frac{\beta R_c}{r_{be}+(1+\beta)R_e}\)

\(R_i=R_e+\frac{r_{be}}{1+\beta }\)
